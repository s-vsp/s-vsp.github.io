## Abstract

This survey reviews sequencing by hybridization, a DNA sequencing approach based on reconstructing a sequence from shorter fragments. It summarizes classical SBH and later variants, with emphasis on computational formulations, algorithmic strategies, complexity issues, and the impact of hybridization errors. The paper positions SBH as a historically important and still useful low-cost method for selected sequencing scenarios.
