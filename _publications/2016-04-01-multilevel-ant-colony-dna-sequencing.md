## Abstract

This article applies multilevel ant colony optimization to DNA sequencing by hybridization, including classical and isothermic variants with multiplicity information. The method treats reconstruction as a combinatorial optimization problem and uses colony-based search to recover DNA sequences from fragment information. The work evaluates the approach for sequencing settings where hybridization data include repeated-fragment multiplicities.
