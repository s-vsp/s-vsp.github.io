## Abstract

This conference paper proposes an end-to-end approach for depression detection from online recordings. The system combines multiple modalities, including facial analysis, emotion-recognition neural networks, and speech processing, to extract signals that may indicate depression. The work frames online multimedia content as a practical source of behavioral features for automated screening-oriented analysis.
