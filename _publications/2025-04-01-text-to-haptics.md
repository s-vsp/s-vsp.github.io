## Abstract

This article explores how touch, audio, speech, and visuals can be combined to make generated stories more immersive. The proposed hybrid AI method analyzes emotional content in text and uses it to decide when and how midair haptic feedback should accompany audiovisual story content generated with latent diffusion methods. A user study with 40 participants suggests that emotionally congruent haptics can increase engagement during short AI-generated multisensory stories.
