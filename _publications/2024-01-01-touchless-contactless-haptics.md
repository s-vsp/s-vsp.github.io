## Abstract

This work presents demonstrators for contactless haptic technologies, including ultrasound, electrostatic piloerection, and chemical stimulation. The demonstrations highlight affective-touch applications such as storytelling, biosignal transfer, and authoring dynamic tactile patterns. The contribution focuses on showing practical interaction possibilities for touch experiences that do not require physical contact.
